This Candidate Privacy Notice explains how Brooklyn Academy (“we,” “us,” or “the Academy”) collects, uses, shares, and protects personal data of individuals who apply for roles or collaborate in recruitment-related activities (“Candidates”). By submitting an application or participating in our hiring process, you acknowledge this Notice.

1. Personal Data We Collect

  • Identification and contact data (name, email, phone, address).
  • Application materials (resume/CV, cover letter, portfolios, references).
  • Professional history (education, roles, skills, certifications, achievements).
  • Interview records and assessment results, including notes from interviewers.
  • Background or eligibility data when permitted by law (work authorization, compliance checks).
  • Technical data from online submissions (device/browser info, IP, logs) for security and fraud prevention.

2. How We Use Candidate Data

  • Evaluate applications, qualifications, and suitability for open roles.
  • Schedule and conduct interviews, tests, or work samples.
  • Communicate about application status, next steps, and feedback where appropriate.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements, including equal opportunity monitoring where applicable.
  • Maintain talent pools for future opportunities, when allowed by law and preferences.
  • Secure our systems, detect fraud, and protect the integrity of the hiring process.

3. Legal Bases

  • Legitimate interests in recruiting and managing talent.
  • Consent where required (e.g., talent pool inclusion, optional demographic data).
  • Compliance with legal obligations (e.g., right-to-work, reporting requirements).
  • Steps taken at your request prior to entering an employment or contractor agreement.

4. How We Share Data

  • Authorized personnel involved in hiring (HR, hiring managers, interviewers, leadership).
  • Service providers supporting recruiting (ATS providers, assessment platforms, communication tools) under contractual safeguards.
  • References or educational institutions you authorize us to contact.
  • Government authorities or regulators when legally required.
  • Other entities in a corporate transaction (merger, acquisition, restructuring) subject to appropriate protections.

5. International Transfers

Where data is transferred across borders, we implement safeguards such as standard contractual clauses, and limit access to personnel with a need to know, consistent with applicable law.

6. Data Retention

We retain candidate data for the duration of the hiring process and for a reasonable period afterward to maintain records, defend legal claims, or consider you for future roles where permitted. If you are hired, relevant data becomes part of your personnel record and is processed under our employee privacy notices.

7. Your Choices and Rights

  • You may access, update, or correct your application information where available.
  • You may object to or restrict processing in certain situations, or withdraw consent where relied upon.
  • You may request deletion subject to legal and legitimate business needs to retain certain records.
  • Depending on your location, you may have additional rights (e.g., portability, non-discrimination, appeal of automated decisions).

8. Security

We apply administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect candidate data. However, no system is fully secure; please share only what is necessary for your application.

9. Updates to this Notice

We may update this Notice periodically. Material changes will be posted in our careers materials or communicated through the contact details you provide. Continued participation in the hiring process after changes indicates acceptance.

10. Contact

For questions about this Notice or to exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@brooklynacademy.com.
