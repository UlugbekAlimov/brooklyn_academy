This Cookie Policy explains how Brooklyn Academy (“we,” “our,” or “the Academy”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website, LMS, and related digital services.

By continuing to browse or use our services, you agree to the placement and use of cookies as described below. You can manage your preferences at any time through your browser settings.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help our site remember your preferences, keep sessions secure, and improve performance and analytics.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Strictly Necessary

  • Authentication and session continuity for the LMS
  • Security protections and fraud prevention
  • Core site functionality (navigation, form submission)

2.2 Performance & Analytics

  • Aggregated usage stats (pages visited, time on page)
  • Site reliability, loading speed, and error tracking
  • Improving content relevance and UX

2.3 Preferences

  • Language and region choices
  • Saved form inputs where applicable
  • Theme or display preferences

3. How We Use Cookie Data

  • Maintain secure logins and protect accounts
  • Deliver requested pages and course materials efficiently
  • Measure engagement to improve curriculum and site usability
  • Diagnose technical issues and prevent abuse

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some analytics, hosting, and communication providers may place their own cookies. Each provider maintains its own cookie and privacy policies, and we require appropriate data-protection standards. We do not allow third parties to use cookies for advertising on our platform.

5. Managing Your Preferences

  • Browser controls: block, delete, or limit cookies in your browser settings
  • Opt-outs: some analytics tools provide their own opt-out mechanisms
  • Impact: blocking certain cookies may affect login, forms, or course access

6. Data Retention

Cookies are stored for durations aligned with their purpose—ranging from session-only to limited persistent periods. We regularly review and minimize retention wherever possible.

7. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our services. Any updates will be posted on this page with the Effective Date adjusted.
